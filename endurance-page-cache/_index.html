ABOUT SALLFEST

Shine A Little Love Fest, is a live music experience that shines love on various artists while providing a platform of awareness for other non-profits.

Inspired by Brian Collins’ Top 10 single, “Shine A Little Love,” get ready for the 4th Annual Shine a Little Love Fest at Sea November 9th -12th 2018! Join in on the music, fun and festivities! Bring your friends and meet many new ones as we Shine the Love on Royal Caribbean’s Mariner Of The Seas out in the open waters of the Atlantic as we cruise from Miami to CocoCay and Nassau with some amazing music from artists such as #1 singer-songwriters and Grammy Nominees and winners Shawn Mullins, Sonia Leigh, Wyatt Durrette, and Levi Lowrey. Also performing during the weekend are Daniel de Los Reyes (of Zac Brown Band), Lilly Winwood, The Van Lears, Granville Automatic, Zach Nytomt, Kristopher James, Alex Saunders, Tom O’Connor, Patrick Blanchard, Magno and Lance & Lea. Serving as host and creator of the festival, Brian Collins will also treat the ticket holders to a performance Saturday night. It will be a weekend at sea to remember!

About Royal Caribbean’s Mariner of The Seas:

This isn’t your same-old, never bold weekend. This is a boredom busting getaway for the whole crew. This summer, Mariner of the Seas® debuts all-new features, with more ways to play on the most maxed-out short getaway ever. Launch yourself into a virtual world on a new bungee trampoline experience — Sky PadSM. Hang ten on the newly added FlowRider® surf simulator or race down The Perfect StormSMwaterslides. Bond over family-style Tuscan eats at Jamie’s Italian by Jamie Oliver, or kick off date night at The Bamboo Room. Ditch the road trip for an action-packed ship. Come Seek the Royal Caribbean®.
